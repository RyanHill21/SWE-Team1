    About the Project

    Our product is a web-based application designed to help students efficiently manage and improve their studying habits. It provides a dashboard where users can log study sessions, monitor their progress over time, and receive personal insights to boost productivity.

    The app targets students who struggle with time management, balancing multiple classes, jobs, and extracurriculars. We address inconsistent study habits, poor scheduling, and low motivation by providing user analytics, progress tracking, and study recommendations.

    Competitors include paper planners and apps like Athenify, MyStudyLife, and Trello. Our differentiator is AI-driven tips & strategies based on real usage. This is a WIP; AI calls are stubbed out with TODOs.
